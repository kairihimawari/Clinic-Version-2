About Pregnancy Age

Pregnancy age, also known as gestational age, is the number of weeks that have passed since the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP). A typical pregnancy lasts about 40 weeks, starting from the LMP. Healthcare providers often use the LMP method to estimate the due date because it is easier to track than the actual date of conception.

Knowing your pregnancy age helps monitor the baby's development and schedule necessary prenatal care. Use the calculator below to estimate your pregnancy age and expected due date.
